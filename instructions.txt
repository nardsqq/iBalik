1. Open dblfs.mwb
2. Once loading is done, hit ctrl+g to forward engineer the said database model.
3. Run xampp, start mysql.
4. Check and modify .env to your database/server settings.
5. Open terminal/git-bash/cmd.
6. Run "php artisan serve"
7. Type "localhost:8000" on a browser tab and it'll redirect you into the homepage of the system.
